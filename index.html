About Us

Tortilleria Rita has been following the same recipe and process for over 30 years; starting in Villahumada, Chihuahua, Mexico, the town is best known for their delicious burritos with asadero cheese.
We believe that using traditional ingredients and equipment is what gives us the best quality in a flour tortilla.
As part of the family tradition, we keep using industrial 'comales' to cook our tortillas and bring them to your table so you can experience what a real flour tortilla tastes like.

We strive for the best quality, flavor, and texture in a tortilla..
