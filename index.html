About Us

Tortilleria Rita proudly upholds a cherished legacy, rooted in over three decades of unwavering commitment to authenticity and excellence. Originating in Villahumada, Chihuahua, Mexico, renowned for their beloved asadero cheese.
We firmly believe that the secret to superior quality lies in honoring time-honored recipes and methods. That's why we meticulously adhere to traditional ingredients and utilize time-tested equipment in our production process. Our continued use of industrial 'comales' —a staple of family tradition— ensures that each tortilla maintains its distinctive texture and flavor profile, faithfully delivering an authentic taste of Mexico to your table.
At Tortilleria Rita, our unwavering pursuit of excellence extends beyond tradition. We are dedicated to achieving nothing less than the pinnacle of quality, flavor, and texture in every tortilla we produce. It's our way of ensuring that each bite delights the senses and transports you to the heart of our culinary heritage.

We strive for the best quality, flavor, and texture in a tortilla..

Our Products

  • Taco size flour tortillas

    Discover the essence of authentic Mexican flavors with our small flour tortillas, perfectly crafted for tantalizing tacos. Made with premium flour and a touch of traditional expertise, these soft and pliable tortillas are ideal for wrapping around savory fillings. Bite-sized yet bursting with taste, they're a must-have for taco nights and fiestas alike!

  • Original flour tortillas

    Indulge in the ultimate comfort food experience with our large-sized flour tortillas, perfect for crafting mouthwatering burritos or cheesy quesadillas. Crafted with care using premium flour and a dash of culinary expertise, these generously sized tortillas offer a soft, pillowy texture that envelops your favorite fillings with every bite

  • Salsas

    Experience the vibrant flavors of Mexico with our traditional Mexican salsa. Bursting with the freshest tomatoes, onions, cilantro, and fiery chili peppers, our salsa embodies the essence of authentic Mexican cuisine.

  • Burritos

    Handcrafted with care and expertise, each burrito is a symphony of savory ingredients, from tender meats to vibrant vegetables, all enveloped in the warm embrace of our traditional flour tortillas.
